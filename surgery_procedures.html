    OUR SERVICE

    Service

    DOG
    Your dog should reach sexual maturity at around 6-7 months old
    There are several advantages of neutering that your dog can benefit from such as:

    Removes the risk of an unplanned pregnancy.

    Prevents testicular tumours and reduces the risk of prostate cancer and other infections.

    Prevents your pet straying away from home in search of a mate.

    Decreases the possibility of tumours and hernias around the bottom, which are common in older, un-neutered dogs.

    CAT
    Your dog should reach sexual maturity at around 6 months of ages
    There are several advantages of neutering that your dog can benefit from such as:

    Stopping their naughty behaviour of spraying urine to mark their territory.

    Reducing the likelihood for them to stray from home.

    A decreased risk of your female cat developing a womb (uterine) infection.

    A reduced risk of getting certain diseases, including feline leukaemia, mammary (breast) cancer and feline AIDS.

    Increasing the chances of them being friendlier and loving.
